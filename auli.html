About

Dotted with the apple orchards, old oaks and pine trees there is no dearth of natural beauty in Auli. Apart from skiing you can also go for numerous treks in the hills of Garhwal Himalayas and enjoy the spellbinding views of the snow-draped mountains.

Auli is a popular hill resort in the Himalayan range dating back to 8th Century AD..

Heaven before the road to heaven

Auli travel blog: Heaven before the road to heaven!

It was quite difficult for me to put an impeccable title to this travel guide. Not because I don’t have enough knowledge about Auli, but because, Auli sets such an unexampled display of nature’s wonders that it left me wordless. True speaking, I am running out of the words… A few mere words cannot describe Auli’s unimaginable nature. But what’s so interesting in Auli? I’ll tell you in this Auli travel blog.

As I began reminiscing about my trip to Auli, all the things that I experienced in Auli have made me nostalgic. Absolutely, Auli is a kind of nostalgia, which is too great. I remember almost every person I met on my way, nature and the moments I’ve experienced in Auli. I also remember the sweetness and the sadness which I’ve encountered in my visit to Auli. Yes, there’s a little sadness too. Isn’t it surprising! I’ll discuss it later in this post, but first, allow me to guide you through my Auli travel blog.
